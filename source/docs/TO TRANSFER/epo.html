EPO Dossier Badges

Dossier badges can be made either as a link a (if theyâ€™re standalone and meant to be clickable), as a div (standalone but not clickable) or span (used inside a bigger link):

<a/div/span [ href="#"] class="application-number type-ep phase-search">
  <b class="phase" title="Search">S</b>
  <span class="number">EP 1234 567 89</span>
  <i class="language">EN</i>
</a/div/span>

The first set is EP dossiers in different phases & different primary languages. They have class .type-ep, and the following phase-specific classes:
.phase-search, .phase-examination, .phase-opposition, .phase-appeal, .phase-limitation
